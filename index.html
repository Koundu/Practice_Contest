The Beagle Blog

The beagle is a breed of small hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world.
